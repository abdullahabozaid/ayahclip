# Theme

## Product direction

The design system is **Midnight Mihrab**: quiet, editorial, reverent, and cinematic. Avoid generic AI gradients, neon purple, glass-card excess, oversized dashboard headings, and decorative clutter.

## Core colors

- Ink: `#08090d`
- Ink deep: `#050507`
- Gold: `#c9a24b`
- Gold soft: `#e0c074`
- Gold deep: `#a07d2c`
- Parchment: `#ece7da`
- Muted: `#a9adbd`
- Muted deep: `#777d95`
- Hairlines: warm gold or parchment at low opacity

## Typography

- Product/UI: Outfit.
- Display: Marcellus, with Cormorant Garamond available for editorial accents.
- Translation presets: Outfit for crisp utility, Lora for readable literary text, Playfair Display for editorial/cinematic text, Cinzel only for restrained title treatment.
- Quran Arabic: Uthmanic Hafs / Amiri Quran family. Preserve the calligraphic face at normal weight; create emphasis with size, contrast, outline, glow, and background treatment rather than synthetic bolding.

## Shape and depth

- Use 12–18px radii for app panels and cards, pill radii only for chips and compact actions.
- Surfaces should be opaque or subtly translucent, separated by hairlines and tonal contrast.
- Gold is an accent and state color, not a large background fill.

## Motion

- Fast, restrained transitions around 160–240ms.
- Verse introductions may fade, blur, slide, or scale, but default templates should favor subtle fades.
- Respect reduced-motion preferences.

## Caption treatments

- Soft white glow: light warm text with a restrained dark outline/shadow, never a fuzzy bloom that reduces Arabic legibility.
- AyahClip gold line: Uthmanic Arabic with a muted olive-gold active-line plate and small warm-white serif translation beneath.
- Split fade: a true black left reading panel that gradually reveals reciter video from the center to the right.
- Clean ink: black field, crisp white Arabic, small translation, minimal effects.
