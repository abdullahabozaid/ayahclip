Updated: 2026-07-17

This audit keeps the original scope intact: Arabic typography, split reciter
composition control, solid/gradient control, Quran range recognition, automatic
alignment, and a wider market-readiness review. A feature is counted only when
its saved-project, preview, and export paths agree.

## Current quantitative gates

- Detection helpers: **18/18 passed**.
- Isolated real-audio recognition: **73 cases**, **43 safe auto-applies**,
  **1.000 auto-apply precision**, **1.000 top-candidate recall**, **zero false
  automatic ranges**.
- Real-audio alignment: **13/13 passed**, including hold-out reciters, run-on
  recitation, phone-band/background stress, non-recitation intro, long ayah,
  mid-surah start, and repeated refrain.
- Unit suite: **248/248 passed**.

  recognition stages, storage quota failure, missing imported-audio recovery,
  and cancel/confirm deletion safety without native dialogs.
- Lint, TypeScript, production build, Vercel production deployment, and live
  HTTP checks passed.

## Remaining evidence gaps

1. The current acoustic weights must not be replaced until a gated refreshed
   model is lawfully obtained and beats the existing release matrix without
   introducing false auto-applies.
2. Add a licensed leakage-free real-handset corpus containing room echo,
   compression, background speech, and reciters absent from model training.
   The repository evaluator now gates those stressor tags separately, minimum
   case/auto-apply coverage, precision, false auto-applies, and recorded license
   provenance, but the audio may not be redistributed in this repository.
3. GitHub's isolated Linux E2E runner rejects `document.fonts.load()` with a
   network error even though Next self-hosts the faces and all other gates pass.
   The test-only correction is to wait for `document.fonts.ready`, use
   non-throwing `document.fonts.check()`, and retain the computed-family and
   canvas-hash distinctness assertions. Product font loading remains strict.

## Completion decision

The creator controls and safety workflow requested in the product are present
and verified. The broader recognition claim is intentionally **not declared
complete** until the licensed unseen-handset/mixed-speech gate is available.
Model output remains a proposal: creators confirm the Quran range and review
every uncertain internal boundary before export.
